Ions

  1. The area stretching between the Bay of Izmit until the Gulluk Bay was called IONIA
  2. The Akas who came from Greece mixed with local people and lived in city states
  3. The main Ion cities were: Ephesus, Milet, Izmir, Foça and Bodrum
  4. The Artemis temple in Ephesus belongs to them
  5. Progressed in naval trade
  6. The most important piece of their literature was the “Illiad and Odessia”
    legend belonging to Homer
  7. Progressed in arts and science. They had Thales and
    Pythagoras in Maths,
    Homer in History, Hippocrate in Medicine and
    Dyogenes in Philosophy
