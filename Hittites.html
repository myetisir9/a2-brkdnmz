Hittites:

  1. Are believed to have come to Anatolia from the Caucasus
  2. Settled around the Halys(Kizilirmak) river. Their capital was Hattusha
  3. Signed the Kadesh Agreement as a result of their war with Egypt over Syria. This is the first written agreement in known history
  4. They had an assembly called Pankus which limited the powers of the king
  5. The most authorized figure after the king was the queen known as Tavananna
  6. They had diaries called Anal, which depicted the lives of the kings and commenced objective history records
  7. They made Stones flat and made reliefs of Gods (the Ivriz and Yazılıkaya(inscribed rock) reliefs belong to them)
  8. They were brought down by the Assyrians
