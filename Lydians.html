Lydians

  1. The area between the Gediz and Menderes rivers was known as Lydia
  2. Their capital was Sard
  3. Progressed in trade, were the first to use money in history
  4. Were influential in the King’s Route that started from Ephesus and stretched until Ninova in Mesopotamia
  5. Were toppled by the Persians
  6. Tumbled easily because their army was made up of merceneries from different clans (couldn’t establish its own constant army)
